Kiki's Delivery Service

Join Kiki on her adventure!

What would it be leave home and learn how to live on your own at thirteen years old?

It's at this age that Kiki moves to a seaside town with her talking cat, Jiji, to spend a year alone, in accordance with her village's tradition for witches in training. After learning to control her broomstick, Kiki sets up a flying courier service and soon becomes a fixture in the community.
